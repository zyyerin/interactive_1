By Erin Zhang, words originally in Japanese, collected here and translated

To be a qualified Chuunibyou one needs to know how to say things in a way hard for most people to understand by using difficult or reliougous words and adding unnecessary discriptions.

Basic words

Eyepatch, Nightmare, chaos, abyss, dark, darkness, left hand aching, the evil eye, god of evil, abstinence, illusion, coat of arms, darkness, wound, one eye

Case Study

I started to learn Germany in 7th grade. Our teacher asked us to pick a Germany name. Next day, I found my friends called themselves things like “Paul”, “Vanessa”, “Tim” and “Daniel.” Yet my name is “dekadenter Engel mit sechs Fluegeln,” meaning “decadent angle with six wings.”

When I was younger, my teacher asked us to name the angle of the geometry, for example 1, 2, a, b, etc. So I named the angle “Tiandixuanghuang Xuanyuan Jian(Baraka Yellow Emperor Sword).”
